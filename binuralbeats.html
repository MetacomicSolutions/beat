How Binaural Beats Work:

Binaural beats are a form of auditory illusion created when two different frequencies are played in each ear. The brain perceives the difference between these frequencies as a "third" sound, known as the binaural beat. This beat can be used to influence brainwave states such as Alpha, Beta, Theta, and Delta waves. Different brainwave states have various uses, such as relaxation, focus, or even deep healing.
